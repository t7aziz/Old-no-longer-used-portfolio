Hi, I'm Taha

I’m a 2nd year student at the University of Waterloo, studying Mechatronics Engineering. Through the program, I happened to stumble upon reasarch based design as a means to solve problems. This is generally considered one of my better stumbles. Outside of design, I enjoy Baroque music, good public transit, and chess.
